A Note Before We Begin

This quiz is a self-reflection tool rooted in the Enneagram tradition — a personality framework with deep roots in wisdom psychology. It's not a clinical diagnosis, and it's not the final word on who you are.

Think of it more like a mirror: it reflects what you show it, and the more honestly you answer, the more you'll see.

Trust your gut. There's no right or wrong answer — only your answer.

This quiz is for self-awareness and reflection only. No personal data is stored or shared.
