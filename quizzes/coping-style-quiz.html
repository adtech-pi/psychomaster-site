A Note Before We Begin 💛

This quiz is a self-reflection tool, not a clinical diagnosis.

The questions are designed to help you notice patterns in how you respond to stress — patterns you might already sense but haven't had the words for. There's no "right" answer, and no result will define you.

That said, if you're going through something really hard right now — a crisis, intense emotional pain, or thoughts of hurting yourself or others — this quiz isn't the place to be. Please reach out to a trusted person or a mental health professional. You deserve real support, not a quiz.

How Do You Really Cope?

Discover your primary coping strategy under stress.

Stress shows up everywhere — in the job you didn't sign up for, the conversation you can't stop replaying, the loss that doesn't make sense, the uncertainty that won't lift.

And everyone has a way of dealing with it.

This quiz isn't about whether your coping style is "good" or "bad." It's about understanding the instinct that rises up in you when things get hard — and what that instinct says about you.

Over the next few minutes, we'll walk through 12 real-life moments. For each one, pick the response that feels most like you. Not the one you think you should pick. The real one.

By the end, you'll get a picture of your dominant coping strategy — and maybe a few surprises along the way.
